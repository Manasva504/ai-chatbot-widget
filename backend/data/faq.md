# Bloom & Co Bakery — Frequently Asked Questions

> **Note:** This is demo content for a fictional bakery. When deploying this
> widget for a real client, replace everything in this file with their actual
> FAQ content. The chatbot only answers from what is written here.

## Opening Hours

- Tuesday to Friday: 7:30 AM – 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
- Sunday: 8:00 AM – 1:00 PM
- Monday: Closed

We are closed on public holidays. Holiday hours are posted on our Instagram
(@bloomandcobakery) a week in advance.

## Delivery

- We deliver within a 10 km radius of the bakery.
- Delivery fee is a flat ₹49.
- **Free delivery** on all orders above ₹999.
- Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 12:00 PM.
- We deliver Tuesday through Sunday between 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM.

## Custom Cake Orders

- Custom cakes require a minimum of **3 days' notice**.
- Wedding and multi-tier cakes require **2 weeks' notice**.
- Custom cake prices start at ₹1,200 for a 1 kg cake; final price depends on
  design complexity.
- A 50% advance payment is required to confirm a custom order.
- To order, call us at +91 98765 43210 or message us on WhatsApp.

## Payment Methods

We accept:
- Cash
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- Bank transfer for large/corporate orders

## Eggless Options

Yes! About half of our menu is eggless, and **any** of our cakes can be made
eggless on request at no extra charge. Eggless items in the display case are
marked with a green dot. We do not currently offer vegan or gluten-free
options.

## Refunds & Cancellations

- Regular orders: cancel up to 24 hours before pickup/delivery for a full
  refund.
- Custom cake orders: the 50% advance is refundable only if you cancel at
  least 48 hours before the delivery date.
- If there is a problem with your order (wrong item, quality issue), contact
  us within 24 hours with a photo and we will replace it or issue a full
  refund.
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment
  method.
